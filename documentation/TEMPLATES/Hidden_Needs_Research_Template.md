# Deep Research: Hidden Needs for [Client Name]

## 1. The "Leak" (Financial)
- **The Gap:** Businesses often miss 10-20% of project value because they don't document "extras" correctly for invoicing/insurance.
- **AI Infra Idea:** **The Auto-Documentation Agent.** 
  - **How:** PM snaps a photo of extra work. Agent analyzes the photo, identifies the line item, and drafts the request instantly.

## 2. The "Quality Loop" (Operational)
- **The Gap:** Quality control is manual. Missed details lead to warranty costs.
- **AI Infra Idea:** **The Vision-Based QC Agent.**
  - **How:** Crew uploads close-out photos of critical points. Vision Agent checks against standard criteria.

## 3. The "Intelligence" (Sales)
- **The Gap:** Waiting for calls after a triggering event (e.g., storm, new home sale). Competitors are already acting.
- **AI Infra Idea:** **The Event-Pulse Agent.**
  - **How:** Connects to Event/Weather data. Auto-sends a check-in SMS to all past clients in an affected radius.

## 4. The "Crew Liaison" (Internal)
- **The Gap:** Office speaks one language; crews speak another. Instructions get lost.
- **AI Infra Idea:** **The Real-Time Bridge Agent.**
  - **How:** Translates office notes to the crew's native language, and translates crew replies back to the office's language in the CRM.

## 5. The "Review-to-Referral" Engine (Growth)
- **The Gap:** They get a 5-star review and stop. They miss the "Neighbor Referral."
- **AI Infra Idea:** **The Neighborhood Dominator Agent.**
  - **How:** When a 5-star review is posted, it identifies the property address and triggers a hyper-local ad or mailer sequence to closest neighbors.

## 6. The "Price Watchdog" (Procurement)
- **The Gap:** Material prices fluctuate. They quote today, but buy in 3 weeks. Margins shrink.
- **AI Infra Idea:** **The Margin-Guard Agent.**
  - **How:** Monitors local supplier pricing. If prices spike, it alerts the owner to adjust quotes or buy immediately.
