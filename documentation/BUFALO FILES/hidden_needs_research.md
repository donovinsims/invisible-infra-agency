# Deep Research: Hidden Needs for Bufalo Contracting

## 1. The "Supplement Leak" (Financial)
- **The Gap:** Contractors often miss 10-20% of claim value because they don't document "supplements" (extra materials/labor found during the job) correctly for insurance adjusters.
- **AI Infra Idea:** **The Auto-Supplement Agent.** 
  - **How:** PM snaps a photo of a rotted deck board or extra layer of shingles. Hermes analyzes the photo, identifies the line item in Xactimate (industry standard), and drafts the supplement request email to the adjuster instantly.
  - **Hidden Need:** They don't know they are leaving $2k-$5k per job on the table due to poor documentation speed.

## 2. The "Subcontractor Quality Loop" (Operational)
- **The Gap:** Bufalo uses crews (likely subs). Quality control is manual. If a crew misses a flashing detail, it's a leak 2 years later (warranty cost).
- **AI Infra Idea:** **The Vision-Based QC Agent.**
  - **How:** Crew must upload "Close-out Photos" of 5 critical points (valleys, flashing, drip edge). Hermes Vision checks these against "Excellence is Our Edge" standards. If it fails, the crew doesn't get the "Job Complete" SMS until it's fixed.
  - **Hidden Need:** Reducing long-term warranty liability and ensuring "Excellence" isn't just a slogan.

## 3. The "Storm-Chaser Intelligence" (Sales)
- **The Gap:** They wait for calls after a storm. Competitors are already knocking doors.
- **AI Infra Idea:** **The Storm-Pulse Agent.**
  - **How:** Connects to NOAA/Weather MCP. When a hail/wind event hits a specific zip code in Winnebago County, Hermes pulls the "Customer Map" from the CRM and auto-sends a "Check-in" SMS to all past clients in that radius: "Hey [Name], big hail just hit your area. Want us to swing by for a free inspection tomorrow?"
  - **Hidden Need:** Proactive retention and "first-mover" advantage after weather events.

## 4. The "Bilingual Crew Liaison" (Internal)
- **The Gap:** Office speaks English; many crews speak Spanish. Instructions get lost in translation.
- **AI Infra Idea:** **The Real-Time Bridge Agent.**
  - **How:** Office posts job notes in English. Hermes translates and sends to the Crew Lead via WhatsApp in Spanish. Crew Lead replies in Spanish; Hermes translates back to the PM in the CRM.
  - **Hidden Need:** Reducing "re-work" caused by language barriers.

## 5. The "Review-to-Referral" Engine (Growth)
- **The Gap:** They get a 5-star review and stop. They miss the "Neighbor Referral."
- **AI Infra Idea:** **The Neighborhood Dominator Agent.**
  - **How:** When a 5-star review is posted, Hermes identifies the property address. It then triggers a "Hyper-Local" ad or mailer sequence to the 20 closest neighbors: "We just finished [Name]'s roof on [Street]. Want a free inspection while we're in the neighborhood?"
  - **Hidden Need:** Turning one job into a "cluster" of jobs in the same area (saves on travel/logistics).

## 6. The "Material Price Watchdog" (Procurement)
- **The Gap:** Shingle prices fluctuate. They quote a job today, but buy materials in 3 weeks. Margins shrink.
- **AI Infra Idea:** **The Margin-Guard Agent.**
  - **How:** Monitors local supplier pricing (via web search/API). If prices spike, it alerts the owner to "Buy Now" for pending contracts or adjust the "Buffer" in new quotes.
  - **Hidden Need:** Protecting net margins in an inflationary environment.
